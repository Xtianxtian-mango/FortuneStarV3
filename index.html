Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lending services.

How do I apply?

To apply for a loan, simply fill out our online application form and submit it. Our team will review your application and get back to you with a decision.

What are the eligibility criteria?

To be eligible for a loan, you must meet certain criteria such as having a steady source of income and a good credit score. Our team will assess your eligibility based on these factors.

How long does the approval process take?

The approval process typically takes a few business days. However, the exact time may vary depending on the complexity of your application and the volume of applications we receive.

What is the interest rate?

The interest rate for our loans is competitive and based on various factors such as your creditworthiness and the loan amount. Please contact our team for more information.

Can I repay early?

Yes, you can repay your loan early without any prepayment penalties. We encourage responsible borrowing and offer flexibility in repayment options.
